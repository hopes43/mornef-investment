AN INSIGHT INTO MORNEF INVESTMENTS (PVT) LTD

MORNEF Investments (PVT) Ltd was incorporated in Zvishavane in 2012 as a registered Building, Civil and Structural Engineering Services Company.

MORNEF Investments (PVT) Ltd is in the highest grading with no upper limit in the value of works in the Public Sector that can be contracted to a contractor. The company is a registered member of the National Employment Council for the Construction Industries Zimbabwe. It is affiliated to the Construction Industry Federation of Zimbabwe (CIFOZ).

The company, with an asset base that includes US $2 million worth of immovable assets and US $1.2 million worth of plant and equipment and highly qualified and experienced personnel, offers quality, timeous and cost effective services in the Construction and Civil Engineering Industry.

Our Story

Mornef Investments (Pvt) Ltd is a premier land development company specializing in civil engineering and infrastructural development of residential land in Zvishavane, Gwanda, and Beitbridge, Zimbabwe.

The company has formed Public Private Partnership (PPP) with Beitbridge Town Council, Municipality of Gwanda and Zvishavane Town Council towards enhancing economic growth, value addition & beneficiation and also the aspirations of the national economic blueprint, Zimbabwe Agenda for Sustainable Socio-Economic Transformation (ZIMASSET).

The principal services lie under the norms of land development and is mandated to offer housing service delivery. For the organisation to achieve this mandate it has operated under a structured chassis which has different levels of multi-faceted expertise required to out-take them.
