About Mornef

The company has formed Public Private Partnership (PPP) with Beitbridge Town Council, Municipality of Gwanda and Zvishavane Town Council towards enhancing economic growth, value addition & beneficiation and also the aspirations of the national economic blueprint, Zimbabwe Agenda for Sustainable Socio-Economic Transformation (ZIMASSET).

The principal services lie under the norms of land development and is mandated to offer housing service delivery. For the organisation to achieve this mandate it has operated under a structured chassis which has different levels of multi-faceted expertise required to out-take them.
